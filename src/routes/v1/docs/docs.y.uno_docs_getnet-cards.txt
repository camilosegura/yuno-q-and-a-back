Integration configuration      Credentials and steps to obtain them:              Ask your Getnet TAM for "Digital Platform (idempotency)" credentials.              Dashboard configuration:   Log in to dashboard.y.uno. Click Connections and then select Getnet. Introduce Name, Client ID, Client Secret and Seller ID. Click Connect     Configuration of webhooks in partner:              Ask the Getnet TAM to configure the webhooks URL.
