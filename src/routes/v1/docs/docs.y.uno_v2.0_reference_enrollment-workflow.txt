Yuno gives customers the possibility to enroll a payment method in order to use it for future purchases and have a seamless payment experience. That payment method enrollment can be used either in our Full or Lite payment implementation and could have one of the following statuses.  Workflow  Payment method status StatusDescriptionCREATEDInitial state at the time of creating a payment method.EXPIREDThe payment method has been expired.REJECTEDYuno has rejected the payment method enrollment.READY_TO_ENROLLThe payment method is available for enrollment.DECLINEDThe payment method has been declined.CANCELEDThe payment method has been canceled.ENROLL_IN_PROCESSThe payment method enrollment is being processed.ENROLLEDThe enrollment of the payment method has been successful.UNENROLL_IN_PROCESSThe payment method unenrollment is being processed.UNENROLLEDThe payment method has been unenrolled.Table of Contents Overview Workflow Payment method status
